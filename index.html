The Weeknd

THE WEEKND

Abel Makkonen Tesfaye — known as The Weeknd — is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer who redefined the intersection of R&B, pop, and dark art pop. His music blends hedonism, existential dread, and cinematic grandeur. From the raw mixtape trilogy to stadium-filling anthems, his arc is unmatched in modern music.

I can't feel my face when I'm with you, but I love it. — Can't Feel My Face, Beauty Behind The Madness

Playboi Carti

Jordan Terrell Carter — Playboi Carti — is the avant-garde prince of punk-trap. His music weaponizes minimalism: ad-libs as instruments, production as atmosphere, and aesthetic as identity. From the self-titled debut to the vampire-punk opus Whole Lotta Red, Carti is a genre unto himself.

I got the ice, I got the drip, I'm feelin' myself tonight. — Magnolia, Playboi Carti

Travis Scott

Jacques Berman Webster II — Travis Scott (La Flame, Cactus Jack) — is a Houston rapper, producer, and cultural architect. His psychedelic, distorted trap soundscapes create full-world experiences. From Rodeo's storytelling to Astroworld's nostalgia-drenched sonic amusement park, Travis transforms music into mythology.
